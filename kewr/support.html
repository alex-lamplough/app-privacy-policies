Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a QR code?
1. Tap "Create QR Code" on the home screen
2. Choose your QR type (URL, Text, Phone, Email, WiFi, or Calendar)
3. Enter your information
4. Customize the style (color, shape, border)
5. Tap "Generate QR Code"
6. Name and save your QR code!
What types of QR codes can I create?
KewR supports 6 types of QR codes:
  • URL - Website links
  • Text - Plain text messages
  • Phone - Phone numbers that open in the dialer
  • Email - Email addresses with optional subject and body
  • WiFi - Network credentials for easy WiFi sharing
  • Calendar - Event details that can be added to calendars
How many QR codes can I save?
  • Free: 5 QR codes
  • Premium: 50 QR codes
  • Supporter: Unlimited QR codes + exclusive app icons
You can upgrade anytime by tapping the upgrade prompt or going to Settings â†’ Plan.
How do I share my QR codes?
After generating a QR code, you can:
  • Share: Tap the Share button to send via Messages, Email, AirDrop, etc.
  • Download: Tap Download to save to your Photo Library
  • View Later: Find all saved QR codes in "Saved QR Codes"
Can I customize the appearance of my QR codes?
Yes! KewR offers extensive customization:
  • Color: Choose any color for your QR code
  • Dot Shape: Square, Rounded, or Circular dots
  • Corner Style: Various corner shapes
  • Border: Add a decorative cartoon-style border
Can I restore my purchases on another device?
Yes! Your Premium or Supporter tier syncs automatically via your Apple ID. On your new device, simply sign in with the same Apple ID and your tier will be restored automatically.
How do I delete a saved QR code?
1. Tap "Saved QR Codes" on the home screen
2. Tap on the QR code you want to delete
3. Scroll down and tap "Delete QR Code"
4. Confirm deletion
Does KewR collect my data?
No. We never collect, store, or transmit your QR code content or personal data. Everything happens locally on your iPhone. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Can I change the app icon?
Yes! Supporter tier users get access to exclusive app icons. Go to Settings â†’ App Icon to browse and select your favorite design.
Why won't my QR code scan?
If a QR code won't scan, try:
  • Ensure good lighting and a stable hand
  • Make sure the QR code is clear and not pixelated
  • Avoid extreme customization that might affect scannability
  • Try downloading the high-resolution image instead of screenshotting

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log water intake?
Simply tap the water drop icon on the home screen. You can use quick-add buttons for common amounts (250ml, 500ml, etc.) or enter a custom amount. Your daily progress will update automatically with a beautiful wave animation!
How do I change my daily hydration goal?
Go to Settings â†’ Daily Goal. You can set any custom goal that works for you. Most adults aim for 2-3 liters (64-100 oz) per day, but individual needs vary based on activity level, climate, and health conditions.
How do reminders work?
Wavely can send gentle reminders throughout the day to help you stay on track. Go to Settings â†’ Reminders to enable notifications and customize reminder times. All reminders are scheduled locally on your device - we don't track or collect any data about them.
What is water fasting mode?
Water fasting mode helps you track periods when you're only consuming water (no food). Tap "Start Fast" to begin a timer, and "End Fast" when complete. Wavely will save your fasting history and show motivational messages based on your progress. Always consult a healthcare professional before beginning any fasting regimen.
How do streaks work?
Build a streak by reaching your daily hydration goal each day. The longer your streak, the more trophies you'll earn (3, 7, 14, 30, 60, 90, 180, and 365 days). Streaks reset at midnight in your local timezone.
Can I switch between mL and oz?
Yes! Go to Settings â†’ Units and choose your preferred measurement system. Wavely supports both metric (mL/L) and imperial (oz/cups) measurements.

Can I restore my purchase on another device?
Yes! Your premium status syncs automatically via your Apple ID. On your new device, go to Settings â†’ Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used to make the original purchase.
Does Wavely collect my health data?
No. All your water intake logs, fasting records, and statistics stay on YOUR device. We never collect, store, or transmit any of your health data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Can I delete my water logs?
Yes! You can delete individual entries from the History view by swiping left. You can also reset all data in Settings if you want to start fresh.
Why isn't my data syncing between devices?
Currently, Wavely stores data locally on each device. Only your premium purchase status syncs via Apple ID. Your water logs, streaks, and fasting records remain device-specific for privacy. We may add optional cloud sync in future updates.
Does Wavely integrate with Apple Health?
Not yet, but we're considering adding this feature in a future update! This would allow you to sync your hydration data with the Health app. We'll always ask for your permission before accessing Health data.
What are "water equivalents"?
Water equivalents are fun comparisons that show your progress in relatable ways - like "You've drunk enough to fill 3 coffee mugs!" or "That's half a goldfish bowl!" They're meant to make tracking more engaging and help visualize your achievement.

Hydration Tips

  • Start your day with a glass of water to kickstart hydration
  • Keep a water bottle visible as a reminder to drink regularly
  • Drink more during exercise, hot weather, or illness
  • If you're thirsty, you're already mildly dehydrated - drink up!
  • Pale yellow urine indicates good hydration
  • Listen to your body - everyone's needs are different

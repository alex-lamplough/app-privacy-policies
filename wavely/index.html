Wavely is designed with privacy at its core. Your health and hydration data stays on YOUR device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your personal information or health data.

Information We DON'T Collect

We take pride in what we don't do:

  • No personal information - We don't collect your name, email, phone number, or any identifying information
  • No health data - Your water intake, fasting records, and health statistics never leave your device
  • No usage analytics - We don't track how you use the app
  • No advertising data - We don't serve ads or collect advertising identifiers
  • No location data - We don't access or collect your location
  • No user accounts - We don't create accounts or require sign-ups

How Wavely Works

Wavely stores everything locally on your device:

  • When you log water intake, it's saved only to your iPhone's local storage
  • Your hydration goals, streaks, and history stay on your device
  • Fasting timers and records are stored locally
  • No internet connection is required to use any core features
  • All data is stored using Apple's secure UserDefaults framework

Notifications

Wavely uses local notifications for hydration reminders:

  • All notifications are scheduled and delivered locally on your device
  • We do not collect or transmit any notification data
  • You can enable/disable reminders anytime in Settings
  • Notification permissions are managed by iOS

In-App Purchases

Wavely offers premium features through a pay-what-you-want model:

  • All purchases are processed securely by Apple through the App Store
  • We do not collect or store payment information
  • Purchase history is managed by Apple and synced via your Apple ID
  • We only receive confirmation that a purchase was made (no payment details)
  • All payment tiers unlock the exact same features

Data Storage

The only data stored on your device includes:

  • Water intake entries - Date, time, and amount you logged
  • Hydration goals - Your custom daily water goal
  • Fasting records - Start/end times and duration of water fasts
  • Streaks and statistics - Your progress and achievements
  • App preferences - Settings like reminder times and measurement units
  • Purchase status - Whether premium features are unlocked

This data is stored locally using Apple's secure storage mechanisms and is never transmitted to external servers.

Cross-Device Sync

Currently, Wavely does not sync data between devices:

  • Each device maintains its own local data
  • Purchase information syncs via Apple's StoreKit framework only
  • Your premium status will sync across devices signed into the same Apple ID
  • Health data (water logs, fasts, streaks) remains device-specific

Third-Party Services

Wavely does not use any third-party services for:

  • Analytics
  • Advertising
  • Crash reporting
  • Cloud storage
  • User tracking

The only third-party service we use is Apple's StoreKit for processing in-app purchases, which is a required part of the iOS ecosystem.

Apple Health Integration

Wavely does not currently integrate with Apple Health:

  • We do not read from or write to the Health app
  • All data tracking happens within Wavely only
  • Future versions may add optional Health integration with your explicit permission

Children's Privacy

Wavely does not knowingly collect any information from children. The app is designed to be privacy-friendly for users of all ages. Since we don't collect any data, users of any age can safely use Wavely.

Data Deletion

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete individual water entries or fasting records anytime
  • Reset all data in Settings if you want to start fresh
  • Deleting the app removes all local data permanently
  • Since we don't store any data on servers, there's nothing to request deletion of

Security

We implement security best practices:

  • All data is stored using Apple's secure UserDefaults framework
  • No data is transmitted over the network
  • The app runs in Apple's secure iOS sandbox environment
  • Purchase validation uses Apple's StoreKit security features

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any personal data, there's nothing for us to delete, export, or modify. You have complete control:

  • View all your data within the app anytime
  • Delete entries or reset data in Settings
  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Restore purchases on any device signed into your Apple ID

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated through app updates.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I hide a message in a photo?
1. Tap "Hide Message" on the home screen
2. Select a photo from your library
3. Type your secret message
4. Add a password (optional)
5. Tap "Hide Message"
6. Share the photo anywhere!
How do my friends reveal the message?
They need to download HushPic (it's free to reveal messages), tap "Reveal Message", select the photo you sent them, and if you added a password, they'll need to enter it.
What happens after 5 wrong password attempts?
The message locks permanently for security. This prevents people from guessing passwords. If someone forgets the password, you'll need to send the photo again with a new message.
How do credits work?
You need 1 credit to HIDE each message. Revealing messages is always free. You start with 5 free credits. Purchase more in Settings â†’ Purchase Credits.
Can I restore my purchases on another device?
Yes! Your purchases sync automatically via your Apple ID. On your new device, go to Settings â†’ Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID.
Does HushPic collect my data?
No. We never collect, store, or transmit your messages or photos. Everything happens locally on your iPhone. See our Privacy Policy for full details.
Can messages be recovered if I forget the password?
No. We don't store passwords or have any way to recover them. This is by design for your privacy and security. Ask the sender to share the photo again.
What photo formats work?
HushPic works with HEIC (iPhone's default), PNG, and JPEG photos. We recommend HEIC for best results as it's lossless.

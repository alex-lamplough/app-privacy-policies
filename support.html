Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log water intake?
Tap the "Water" card on the home screen to access water tracking. You can use quick-add buttons for common amounts (250ml, 500ml, 750ml, 1000ml) or tap "Custom Amount" to enter a specific quantity. Your progress updates in real-time with beautiful animations!

Tap the profile icon (top right) â†’ Settings â†’ Daily Goal. You can set any custom goal that works for you. Most adults aim for 2-3 liters (64-100 oz) per day, but individual needs vary based on activity level, climate, and health conditions.
How do reminders work?
Tap the "Reminders" button on the home screen to customize notifications. You can:
  • Enable/disable reminders
  • Set hourly reminders (8AM-9PM)
  • Choose specific times throughout the day
  • Enable quiet hours (9PM-7AM) for uninterrupted sleep
All reminders are scheduled locally on your device - we don't track or collect any notification data.
What is water fasting mode?
Water fasting helps you track periods when you're only consuming water (no food). Tap the "Fasting" card on the home screen to:
  • Choose from preset durations (12h, 16h, 18h, 20h, 24h, 36h, 48h, 72h)
  • Set a custom duration
  • Start an open-ended fast with no time limit
  • View motivational messages based on your progress
  • Track milestones and see your fasting history
Always consult a healthcare professional before beginning any fasting regimen.

Wellness Tips

Hydration

  • Start your day with a glass of water to kickstart hydration
  • Keep a water bottle visible as a reminder to drink regularly
  • Drink more during exercise, hot weather, or illness
  • If you're thirsty, you're already mildly dehydrated - drink up!
  • Pale yellow urine indicates good hydration
  • Listen to your body - everyone's needs are different

Fasting

  • Start with shorter fasts (12-16 hours) and gradually increase duration
  • Stay hydrated - water, black coffee, and tea are typically allowed
  • Break fasts gently with easily digestible foods
  • Consult a healthcare professional before starting any fasting regimen
  • Listen to your body - if you feel unwell, it's okay to stop
  • Fasting isn't suitable for everyone (pregnant, certain medical conditions)

Breathwork & Meditation

  • Practice in a quiet, comfortable space
  • Start with short sessions (5-10 minutes) and build up
  • Consistency matters more than duration
  • Morning meditation can set a positive tone for the day
  • Use breathwork to manage stress and anxiety
  • Don't judge your practice - there's no "perfect" meditation
